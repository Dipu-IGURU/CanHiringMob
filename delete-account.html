Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, location
  • Profile Data: Profile photo, bio, job title, resume/CV
  • Application Data: All job applications and application history
  • Saved Jobs: All bookmarked/saved job listings
  • Messages: All messages between you and employers/recruiters
  • Account Settings: All privacy settings and preferences
  • Activity Data: Job search history, profile views, and activity logs

Data Retention Period

Account Deletion: Once your account deletion request is processed, all your personal data and account information will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

Backup Data: Some data may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days for security and recovery purposes, after which it will be permanently removed.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all your data, including job applications, saved jobs, and messages. Please ensure you have downloaded any important information before requesting deletion.

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact us at info@canhiring.com
